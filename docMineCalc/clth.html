Accessory Purpose / Style Tip
Tie / Bow Tie / Neck CravatAdds formality and color. Choose silk for elegance.
Tie Clip / Bar / Tie PinKeeps the tie in place and adds a clean, polished detail.
Pocket SquareInstantly adds flair and contrast to your blazer. Match it with your shirt/tie, not exactly.
Lapel Pin / BroochFor events like weddings or special occasions. Adds uniqueness.
BeltSlim leather belt that matches your shoes (black or brown).
WatchA classic metal or leather-strap watch completes a sophisticated look.
CufflinksFor formal shirts with French cuffs. Choose subtle metals or personalized designs.
SunglassesAviators or square frames work well with suits. Stylish yet sharp.
ShoesFormal leather shoes like Oxfords, Derbies, or Monk Straps. Match with belt.
Other Accessory Purpose / Style Tip
ScarfWool or cashmere in winter - adds warmth and elegance.
Leather GlovesSleek black or brown gloves in colder months.
Briefcase / Leather BagGreat for business or travel - adds professionalism.
Dress SocksGo for subtle patterns or bold colors - avoid plain white.
Perfume / CologneA subtle signature scent makes a lasting impression.
